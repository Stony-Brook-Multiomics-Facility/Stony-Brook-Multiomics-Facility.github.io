# About the Facility

The Stony Brook University Multiomics Facility supports researchers across the university with access to cutting-edge single-cell and spatial sequencing technologies.

## What we offer

We work with investigators at all stages, from experimental design through data analysis and interpretation. Our goal is to make multiomics technologies accessible to researchers regardless of their computational background.
