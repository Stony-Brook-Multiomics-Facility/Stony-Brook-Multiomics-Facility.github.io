# Stony Brook University Multiomics Facility

We provide a comprehensive suite of services for the Stony Brook research community, including single-cell sequencing, spatialomics, data analysis support, training, and consultation.

## Services

- **Single-cell sequencing** — scRNA-seq, ATAC-seq, and multiome workflows
- **Spatialomics** — Spatial transcriptomics and imaging-based methods
- **Data analysis support** — From preprocessing to publication-ready figures
- **Training** — Hands-on courses in computational analysis (see [Courses](/courses))
- **Consultation** — Experimental design and project planning
